Attribution is required
If you are using a song, it is required to properly attribute it in your project's credits.
When attributing music, list the song's title and give credit by name (Abstraction).
Also, you must include a link to either the Bandcamp page (http://abstractionmusic.bandcamp.com/) or home page (http://www.abstractionmusic.com/).
